Triple-band Microstrip Patch Antenna — C-band, Wi-Fi 6E, X-band
  • Single-layer rectangular patch (50×40 mm²) on Rogers RO4003C (εr = 3.55) achieving resonances at 4.141 GHz (C-band), 6.148 GHz (Wi-Fi 6E), and 8.407 GHz (X-band)

  • Only reviewed design covering C-band + Wi-Fi 6E + X-band using a simple rectangular DGS (no metamaterial)
